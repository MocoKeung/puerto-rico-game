## Hero Section

### 🎯 The Game
Puerto Rico puts you in the role of a colonial governor on the island of Puerto Rico during the age of Caribbean prosperity. Your goal: accumulate the most victory points by developing your island, producing goods, and shipping them back to Europe.

Every decision matters. Every role you choose shapes the game. Will you prioritize quick sugar sales or invest in tobacco plantations for long-term gains? Build a diverse economy or specialize? The choice is yours.

## Key Features

### ✨ Why Play Puerto Rico Online?

**🎭 Iconic Role Selection**  
Choose from 7 distinct roles — Settler, Mayor, Builder, Craftsman, Trader, Captain, and Prospector. Each role offers powerful actions, but choose carefully: opponents benefit too!

**🌾 Deep Economic Gameplay**  
Plant corn, indigo, sugar, tobacco, or coffee. Build production chains. Manage your workforce. Master the art of efficient resource management.

**🏛️ Monumental Buildings**  
Construct 18 unique buildings, each offering game-changing abilities. From Small Markets to Customs Houses, every structure shapes your strategy.

**⚔️ Zero-Luck Strategy**  
No dice. No cards to shuffle. Pure skill, pure strategy, pure tension. Your decisions — and your opponents' — determine every outcome.

**🌍 Play Anywhere**  
Beautiful Caribbean-inspired design. Real-time multiplayer. Play with friends across the globe or challenge AI opponents.

## How It Works

### The Four Pillars of Victory

```
1. COLONIZE 🏠
   Plant crops and claim land. Corn for quick growth, 
   coffee for premium profits.

2. PRODUCE ⚙️
   Transform plantations into goods. Chain buildings 
   for maximum efficiency.

3. TRADE 💰
   Sell goods at the trading house. Prices shift based 
   on rarity — timing is everything.

4. SHIP 📦
   Load goods onto ships for victory points. Match 
   demand, maximize returns.
```
